Retro Restaurant Website Idea!



Description

My website will be a retro website. My site is for anyone wishing to get the 90s computer experience or feel nostalgic. It’s also for myself and for fun. I want to get more javascript experience and do cool things with the design. It’ll be a little like my research websites, with the pop-ups looking like old computer windows. It’ll also have flashing comic sans text, cheesy keyboard music, scrolling marquees across the top, mouse trails, and 90s gifs like MC hammer dancing. I also want to do the old fashioned mouse icon and the grey looking windows and buttons. I want to include pixellated snow falling in the background like old cheesy myspace themes. As for the content I will make a hard-to-navigate to about page. I will also include a (somewhat hidden) 90s meme and gif page. Then the rest of the site will be a fake restaurant page. Complete with a menu, hours, fake testimonials, a meet the staff page (with pictures of barbie dolls and animals).
